Here are the Subsidaries of MHR Property Conglomerates Inc.


BAYGAS

Established in 2014, Bay Gas Petroleum Distribution, Inc. is engaged in the refilling, retail, and, distribution of fuel and petroleum products in Cebu City. The establishment is under the umbrella of MHR Properties Conglomerate, Inc. With six (6) local staff directly employed in the station.

CEBIC INDUSTRIES

The company started as CEBIC Trading in February 2000, originally intending to cater to the growing demand for medical supplies and other hospital products in Cebu City.

MHRHCI

The company that started all MHR ventures started to bear the name MHR Health Care, Inc. since its SEC registration in 2018. With MHR's insatiable drive to reach new heights, other successful ventures were launched and MHR Property Conglomerates was created as an umbrella company of all MHR ventures.

VERBENA HOTEL

An excellent 2-star property set 3.1 km away from the 4th SM City Cebu Shopping Mall and located in the center of the city, Verbena Hotel has 45 rooms with balcony and sitting area. This low-budget hotel features a daily breakfast buffet in its cozy restaurant located in the ground floor.
